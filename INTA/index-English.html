Introduction to R Programming

About the training

This training is part of a collaborative initiative between Breeding Insight - Cornell University and INTA Costa Rica. The remote sessions will begin on March 21st 2025 and continue on consecutive Fridays through April 11th. Each session consists of a 2-hour hands-on training workshop. The only prerequisite is having R and RStudio installed on your computer. To help you with the setup process, please refer to the following installation tutorial.

Materials

  • R and RStudio Installation tutorial - Espaol - Portugus - English
  • Theoretical presentation - Espaol - Portugus - English
  • Main tutorial - Espaol - Portugus - English
  • Homework - Espaol - Portugus - English
