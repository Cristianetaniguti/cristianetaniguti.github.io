I have an undergraduate degree in Biotechnology by Federal University of So Carlos (UFSCar), Master degree in Genetics and Plant Breeding (ESALQ/USP). I am under the supervision of Antonio Augusto Franco Garcia and member of Statistical Genetics Lab. I have interest in study computational tools and statistical methods for plant breeding related with molecular biology and quantitative genetics.
